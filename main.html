Part 1 - Overview

This dashboard provides an overview of the distribution of bots and humans across different social media platforms. It highlights the proportion of bot accounts compared to human accounts, as well as the distribution across platforms and language locales.

Key Insights:

  • A significant portion of accounts across all platforms are identified as bots. This might indicate a widespread of artificially created content is found on social media.
  • Language locale distribution shows that English is the most common language among both bot and human accounts, followed by Spanish and Portuguese.
  • Gemini tends to make longer posts. And in general, across all languages and platforms, bots would have longer posts on social media than humans.
